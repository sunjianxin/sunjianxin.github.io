Research Focus

My lab works at the intersection of Data, Human, and AI. My research aims to establish the core foundations of next-generation AI-driven data analysis and visualization systems capable of automatically analyzing large-scale, high-dimensional, and in situ data with enhanced expressivity, explainability, and scalability, empowering AI for Science through accelerated scientific discovery and innovation across specialized and interdisciplinary domains. Current Key research areas include:

  • Neural Rendering through View Synthesis
  • Agentic AI for Autonomous Scientific Discovery
  • AI-Friendly Data Representation
